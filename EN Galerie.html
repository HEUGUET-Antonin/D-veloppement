Feminist singers

Anne Sylvestre

Her real name was Anne-Marie Thérèse Beugras. She was born on 20 June 1934 and died on November 30, 2020. She is a French feminist singer who is known for the songs "Mon mari est parti", "Petit bonhomme" and "Douce maison".

Beyoncé

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles was born on 4 September 1981 and is an R'n'B and pop artist. Her best-known songs songs are "Crazy in Love", "Single Ladies" and "Check on It".

Clara Luciani

Clara Luciani is a French singer and songwriter. She was born on 10 July 1992 in Martigues, She became known through the group La Femme before enjoying solo success. Her best-known songs are "La grenade", "le Reste" and "On ne meurt pas d'amour".

Ariana Grande
