"Brol', a committed album

"Brol" is the first album of the artist Angèle. The title of this album is a Flemish word which means "disorder". This word, chosen by the artist, evokes her childhood and her country, Belgium. It was released on 5 October 2018 and is certified double diamond disc with over 1,000,000 sales to date. During the 2019 Victoires de la Musique, the album received the title of the title of revelation album of the year. This first project of 12 singles by the singer Angèle is very committed and feminist.

A feminist album

Angèle's album includes the feminist single "Balance Ton Quoi" which is the second track on the project. 2nd track of the project. The clip was released on YouTube on 15 April 2019 and was a huge success. Indeed, the video-clip soon reached the hundred million views on the platform.

The title of the song is a direct reference to the feminist movement "Balance Ton Porc"as well as the "Me too" movement. Angèle denounces sexism in this song and "Balance Ton Quoi" quickly became a feminist anthem sung at feminist demonstrations.

More specifically, Angèle directly addresses the men who participate in street harassment, the degradation of women's image and the trivialisation of misogyny.

Social networks

In the seventh track of the album, "Victime des réseaux", Angèle denounces the hypocrisy and jealousy of people on social jealousy of people on social networks. The song also denounces the toxicity of the networks.

Indeed, since the existence of social networks a kind of jealousy between users has developed. Moreover, people on Instagram in particular, share great moments of life, beautiful photos. When we see all these beautiful things we sometimes feel that our life life sucks compared to others.

It is this problem that Angèle denounces in the chorus of her single: "I wish I'd be like you, You wish you'd be like me". Angèle describes the vicious circle of Angèle describes the vicious circle of this jealousy because we will always be jealous of someone, and someone will always be jealous of us.

Finally, in the third track of her album "Jalousie", Angèle addresses the issue of jealousy. In this track, the artist describes jealousy as a harmful feeling in the heart of a love relationship.

Money and popularity

"La thune" is the first track on Angèle's album. It denounces the greed for money and the "fame". The singer describes the world as envious of wealth and popularity. She adds that this envy is the only motivation for people to move in life. A selfish and harmful motivation.

In this single, Angèle once again evokes in her album the toxicity of social networks as people are motivated to move only to be popular.

Support for the LGBT cause
